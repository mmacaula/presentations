Do Custom Elements have an appropriate polyfill?

Which brings me back to custom elements, the point of the talk!

Yup

You have two choices

  1. Webcomponents.org
  2. Document-Register-Element

Comparison

Polyfill Size (min and gzipped) Compatibility
Webcomponents.js 5.1KB IE10+, Current Browsers,
Document-Register-Element 3KB IE8+, Safari, tons of mobile browsers
Webcomponents contains polyfills for a weakmap as well
